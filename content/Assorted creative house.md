A single professional front for a large group of creatives.  Random house takes commissions, which are distributed amongst groups based of people part of the house. Payment for the job is distributed between those who worked on the job, based on a percentage of hours commited to any given job. A percentage of the total project value is taken for use managing the random house.

Members suspected of lying about hours commited to gain a larger cut will be removed
