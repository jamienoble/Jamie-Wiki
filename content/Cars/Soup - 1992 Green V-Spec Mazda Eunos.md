# Soup - 1992 Green V-Spec Mazda Eunos

Soup is a classic 1992 Mazda Eunos Roadster (MX-5), V-Spec edition, finished in British Racing Green. This Japanese import is known for its lightweight design, iconic pop-up headlights, and engaging driving experience.

## Key Details

- **Model:** Mazda Eunos Roadster V-Spec
- **Year:** 1992
- **Colour:** British Racing Green
- **Spec:** V-Spec (tan leather interior, Nardi wood trim, limited-slip differential)
- **Engine:** 1.6L inline-four
- **Transmission:** 5-speed manual
- **Origin:** Japanese import
